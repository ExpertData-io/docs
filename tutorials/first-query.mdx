## Overview

In this tutorial, you'll learn how to:

1. Connect your first database
2. Ask Genie a question
3. View and understand the results

**Time to complete**: 10 minutes

## Step 1: Add a Connector

First, let's connect to your data source.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Connectors">Click **Connectors** in the sidebar</Step>
  <Step title="Create New Connection">Click **+ New Connector**</Step>
  <Step title="Enter Details">
    For PostgreSQL: - **Host**: your-database-host.com - **Port**: 5432 -
    **Database**: your_database_name - **Username**: your_username -
    **Password**: your_password
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test & Save">Click **Test Connection**, then **Save**</Step>
</Steps>

## Step 2: Ask Genie

Now let's ask your first question!

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Home">
    Click **Home** in the sidebar
  </Step>
  <Step title="Type Your Question">
    Try something like:
    > "How many records are in the users table?"
  </Step>
  <Step title="Press Enter">
    Genie will generate a query and show you the results
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 3: Explore Results

Genie's response includes:

- **The Query**: The SQL that Genie wrote for you
- **Results Table**: The data returned
- **Insights**: Any patterns Genie noticed

### Try More Questions

| Question Type | Example                                  |
| ------------- | ---------------------------------------- |
| **Counting**  | "How many orders were placed last week?" |
| **Sums**      | "What was total revenue in January?"     |
| **Grouping**  | "Show orders by status"                  |
| **Filtering** | "Find customers from California"         |
