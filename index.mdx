## Getting Started

New to ExpertEngine? Follow these steps to get up and running:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Your Account">
    Sign up at [app.expertengine.tech](https://app.expertengine.tech/signup) and
    verify your email.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Your First Connector">
    Connect to your database (PostgreSQL, MySQL, BigQuery, and more).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start Asking Questions">
    Open Genie and ask questions like "Show me sales by region last month"
  </Step>
</Steps>
